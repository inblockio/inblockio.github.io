Our Work

Aqua Protocol

Aqua solves digital notarization with an open, decentralized approach—starting with private, local hash-chains that stay yours until you choose to share. Explore it at aqua-protocol.org.

Milestones: Personal Knowledge Container (PKC) integration (2021/22), Guardian integration (Summer 2024), Aquafier launch (aquafier.inblock.io) by end of 2024.
