Trust that travels
with your data.

In a world of deepfakes and autonomous AI, trust should be built into the data itself, not just the platforms that hold it. Aqua embeds cryptographic verification directly into your files, making them portable, permanent, and independently verifiable anywhere.

Stop relying on central servers to prove what happened. Aqua embeds verification directly into your data so it can be checked anywhere, independently. Aqua creates a portable memory for your data.

Aqua Protocol

An open protocol that attaches a cryptographic history to any file. Every edit, every signature, every handoff gets recorded in a hash chain that travels with the data itself. No central server stores the proof. No vendor lock-in. The file is the audit trail.

Portable

Verification data lives inside the file. Move it to another system, another country, another year. It still checks out.

Offline-verifiable

No API call needed. Anyone with the file can verify its full history using only math.

Composable

Aqua chains link together. A signed document can reference the identity chain of the signer and the witness chain of a notary. One protocol, many trust topologies.

Solutions

Aqua isn't one product for one problem. Here's how it maps to what you're already dealing with.

eSignature

Sign a contract and the proof travels with the PDF. No vendor portal needed to verify it later.

Document Signing

Notarize a document once. Forward it ten times. The tenth recipient can still verify the original signer without calling you.

Supply Chain Tracking

A part leaves a factory in Shenzhen. Six handoffs later, the buyer in Munich can verify every step — offline, without trusting any single party in between.

AI Operational Security

Your AI agent approved an invoice at 3 AM. Was it authorized? What data did it see? Aqua gives you the receipts.

Data Provenance

The EU AI Act requires you to document where your training data came from. Aqua records that history as the data moves — not after the fact in a spreadsheet.

Compliance

Auditors ask "prove it." Aqua-stamped data answers that question before you open a ticket with your compliance team.

Financial Services

KYC documents that prove themselves. Transaction records your regulator can verify without asking you for access to your systems.

Healthcare

Patient data shared between hospitals without either side having to "just trust" the other. Research datasets with provenance that holds up to peer review.

Government

Public records that citizens can verify independently. Permits and certificates that don't require calling a government office to confirm they're real.

Manufacturing

Quality certificates that travel with the component. When a batch gets recalled, you trace it back in minutes, not weeks.
